How to Play Poly Track

  • Select Your Vehicle: Start by choosing your vehicle. Poly Track offers a variety of cars, each with different speeds, handling, and abilities. Select the one that suits your racing style to give yourself the best advantage.
  • Choose a Track: Poly Track features a wide range of colorful, polygonal tracks, each with its own set of challenges. From narrow curves to wide-open spaces, each track will test your reflexes and driving skills. Choose a track and get ready to race!

  • Master the Controls: The controls in Poly Track are simple yet responsive. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer your vehicle, accelerate, and brake. Getting the feel of the controls is key to navigating the twists and turns successfully.
